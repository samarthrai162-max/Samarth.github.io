💌 A Special Birthday Card

Someone special has a message waiting for you...

💖 Shayari 1

Har khushi tumhare kadam choome,
Har sapna tumhara sach ho jaaye,
Is khaas din par meri dua hai,
Tumhari har muskaan aur bhi pyaari ho jaaye. ❤️

🌹 Shayari 2

Chaand ki roshni se bhi zyada noor mile,
Phoolon se bhi zyada khushboo mile,
Bhagwan kare tumhe zindagi mein,
Har din nayi khushiyon ka tohfa mile. ✨

✨ Shayari 3

Na ho kabhi khushiyon ki kami,
Na aaye kabhi aankhon mein nami,
Hamesha muskurate raho tum,
Yehi hai dil se meri dua sabhi. ❤️

💕 Shayari 4

Khilte rahein phool tumhari raahon mein,
Chamakte rahein sitaare tumhari baahon mein,
Khushiyon se bhar jaaye har din tumhara,
Yahi dua hai dil ki gehraiyon mein. 🌸

❤️

Happy Birthday Dear ❤️

Tumhari zindagi hamesha khushiyon,
pyaar aur safalta se bhari rahe.

May all your dreams come true and
your smile never fade. ✨
